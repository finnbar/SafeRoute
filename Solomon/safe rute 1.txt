Description:

Do you use Google Maps? Are you sick of unsafe routes? We bring you Safe Route! 
Safe Route it is like Google Maps but offers you a safer alternative. If you walk, cycle or drive then Safe Route will tell a way to go which won't take you through dangerous areas. By using police data we can keep you safe and aware of what's happening on your route.

Key Features:

*Connects to data.police.uk API to provide up to date crime statistics. 
*Free updates
*Connects to Google Maps data to provide reliable directions.
*A secure service.
